Overview
Elevating a luxury leather brand

Who is Stone For Gold?

Stone For Gold is a Singapore-based luxury leathercraft brand specialising in handcrafted crocodile leather goods — watch straps, key fob covers, card holders, belts, and accessories. Their products serve discerning clients who own watches from Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Zenith, and IWC, as well as car owners seeking bespoke key fob covers.

My responsibilities

I worked across the full brand touchpoint — designing festive and promotional social media creatives, producing campaign assets for sale events and workshops, and leading a complete Shopify website revamp. The goal was to establish a visual language that matched the craftsmanship and luxury positioning of the products themselves.

Campaigns, sales & workshops

Festive & promotional creatives

I designed a series of social media campaigns across festive occasions and sale events — from Hari Raya and National Day promotions to mid-year tiered discounts. Each creative was crafted to stop the scroll while staying true to the brand's luxury aesthetic.

Workshops & membership

Beyond product promotions, Stone For Gold runs leathercraft workshops and a members programme. I designed assets for the National Day Couple Special workshop — a 90-minute guided session — alongside membership and additional National Day sale creatives.

"Every creative had to feel premium first — the discount was the detail, not the headline."

Web Design
Shopify website revamp

Homepage & brand credibility

The revamped homepage leads with a full-bleed product hero — a handcrafted key fob cover photographed in a luxury car interior. Immediately below, a clients bar features logos of the luxury watch brands Stone For Gold serves: Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Zenith, and IWC.

Luxury brand client logos — Rolex, Patek Philippe, AP, Zenith, IWC

Before / After showcase

A Before / After section visually demonstrates the transformation Stone For Gold delivers — replacing worn or original straps with handcrafted crocodile leather. The side-by-side format is the most compelling proof of quality a luxury accessories brand can offer.

E-commerce
Reviews, products & checkout

Social proof & product categories

A "Crafted with Passion & Precision" marquee anchors the brand's promise before surfacing a Google Reviews section — 4.9★ across 80+ verified reviews. Below, three product category tiles (Watch Straps, Car Fob Covers, Card Holders) direct buyers to their relevant collections.

Customer reviews (4.9★) and product category navigation

Product listing & detail pages

The product listing page surfaces watch straps with clean photography, pricing, and sale badges — supporting quick comparison across models like Audemars Piguet, Patek Philippe, Apple Watch, and Richard Mille. The product detail page provides full specifications: leather type, edge dye, colour options, and a rich description built for high-consideration purchases.

Streamlined checkout

The Shopify checkout is clean and distraction-free — contact, delivery method, and shipping details on the left, with a clear order summary on the right. The single-page flow reduces drop-off and matches the premium experience customers expect from the brand.
