Overview
Understanding the brief

Who is PeakTop Engineering?

PeakTop Engineering Pte Ltd is a Singapore-based Mechanical & Electrical contractor with over 50 years of combined expertise. They deliver electrical mains installation, air conditioning & mechanical ventilation, and communication & security systems to residential, commercial, and data centre clients across Singapore.

My responsibilities

The client needed a professional online presence that communicated their engineering credentials, showcased their project portfolio of 200+ completed builds, and clearly presented their service offerings. I was responsible for the full website design — from information architecture and visual design through to front-end execution and launch.

Homepage
First impression & social proof

Communicating trust above the fold

The homepage hero leads with a bold value proposition — "We deliver excellence with greater value." — set against real on-site photography to ground the brand in genuine construction work. Below the fold, three featured project categories (Residential, Commercial, Data Centres) immediately signal the breadth of PeakTop's portfolio.

Proof points that convert

A stats band displays the numbers that matter to engineering clients — years of experience, projects completed, and contract value — giving first-time visitors immediate confidence before they dig further. The "What We Offer" section then maps out the three service pillars with accompanying imagery.

Services
Showcasing three core offerings

Engineers with Passion

The services page opens with a hero that sets the tone — competent, passionate, and client-first. Each of the three service lines is given dedicated space with clear scope descriptions, supported by real photography that shows PeakTop's team at work on actual sites.

"Every service section is designed to answer the question a prospective client actually asks: 'Can you handle a project like mine?'"

About
Building credibility & trust

Company story & leadership

The About Us page leads with the company vision — "Deliver Excellence" — shown against a large aspirational imagery of a modern interior PeakTop helped build. A company overview section pairs team photography with a written summary, while the mission section goes deeper into values and approach.

Awards & accreditations

PeakTop holds a BizSafe Star Certificate and is an Ordinary Member of the Singapore Business Federation. These credentials are displayed prominently on the About page to reinforce trust with enterprise and government clients who require certified M&E contractors.

Portfolio
200+ projects, zero clutter

A filterable project gallery

PeakTop's greatest asset is the depth and quality of their completed work. The projects page presents the full portfolio in a structured grid, filterable by category — Residential, Commercial, and Data Centres. Each project tile shows a high-quality site photograph, making the page visually compelling while staying easy to navigate.
