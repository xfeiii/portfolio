Overview
A swipe-based matching app that connects dog owners with trusted, verified walkers.

What is PawSwipe?

PawSwipe reimagines pet care discovery with an interface inspired by swipe-based matching. Dog owners can browse verified walkers, review their profiles, and book confidently — while walkers can find and accept jobs that suit their schedule.

The prototype supports three perspectives: Dog Owner, Dog Walker, and Explorer. My contribution focused on the pet verification system — a closed-community trust layer that ensures every owner and animal on the platform is authenticated before accessing bookings.

How the App Works

From onboarding and verification through to matching, booking, and payment — every screen is designed to reduce friction and build confidence between owners and walkers.

Low-Fidelity
Mapping the verification flow before going into pixels.

Closed Community Design

To create a trusted environment, I designed a closed community where dog owners must verify both themselves and their pets before accessing the platform. Owners submit proof of identity along with relevant pet documents, giving walkers greater confidence before accepting bookings.

The verification flow includes clear success and error states. Successful verification advances users to profile setup, while unsuccessful submissions provide actionable feedback so users know exactly how to re-submit.

High-Fidelity
Verification flow for a secure, trusted pet community.

From Wireframe to Polished UI

The high-fidelity prototype refines the verification journey into a simple, guided experience. Users upload proof of identity (IC or Passport) then submit pet documents such as a Dog Identification Certificate and vaccination records.

Animated feedback screens communicate progress throughout: successful verification unlocks profile setup, while errors provide clear guidance for document resubmission — strengthening trust while encouraging responsible pet ownership.

Every verification screen is built to communicate progress and build trust — ensuring dog owners and walkers feel safe from the very first tap.
